ABOUT NEWS OUTLOOK

Hello there. This site performs rudimentary sentiment analyses of the latest news headlines.

The sentiment is determined by matching indvidual words within the text against AFINN-165. Aggregate sentiment scores of headlines are calculated and displayed as raw numbers or as emoticons. This is not the most sophisticated way to analyse sentiment but it's the best I can do right now ¯\_(ツ)_/¯

AFINN-165

Developed by Danish data scientist Finn Årup Nielsen, AFINN is one of the simplest lexicons for sentiment analysis.

The current version of the lexicon is AFINN-165 and it contains over three thousand words with a polarity score ranging from [- 5] to [5] associated with each word.

Positive words (adjectives, verbs, nouns and exclamations) have positive sentiment scores. While negative words have negative scores. The more emotive the word, the larger its score's value. For instance -

Adorable 3 Hideous - 3
Promote 1 Deny - 2
God 1 Wanker - 3
ROFLMAO 4 WTF - 4

Source:
Finn Årup Nielsen and the Technical University of Denmark (2015)
